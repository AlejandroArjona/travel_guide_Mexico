The magical country

About this country

Colonial cities, Magical Towns and exclusive beaches continue to conquer the hearts of national and international travelers. Traveling is an experience that is always exciting. However, the long period of confinement that we have experienced caused practically all tourist destinations in the country to be closed to visitors for several months. Within the first places were located both colonial cities and the charming Magical Towns. However, the sun and the beach also continue to conquer the desire to spend a few days by the sea. All of them still captivate people willing to pack their clothes in a suitcase and enjoy a vacation with their travel proposal.

México City

It could seem like a city like any other, but with the peculiarity of having certain sights and memorable monuments to its history. Here, you can visit the "Bellas Artes Palace", a place where a large number of artists and celebrities have performed, whether they are music, arts, comedy, and everything that is part of art. You can also visit the angel of independence, which commemorates those events that occurred in 1810 known as the independence movement, the fight that freed Mexico from the Spanish conquerors.

Guanajuato

Guanajuato stands out for its mining past, its underground streets and tunnels, its baroque and neoclassical architecture, its colorful houses, its alleys and its weight in the history of Mexico. While San Miguel de Allende is a jewel of the Mexican baroque with some of the most beautiful buildings in the entire country. If you visit Guanajuato you will meet a lot of wonderful people who love their land and who welcome you with a smile and the hospitality of someone who has known you for a long time. Guanajuatenses are warm, friendly, curious and always have good words for their land.

Jalisco

the Mexico of the colonial squares, the most emblematic places, the indigenous community, the crafts and ceramics, the diversity, the tequila, the sones, the mariachi and the idyllic beaches bathed by the Pacific Ocean. Jalisco is Mexico, as the slogan says and begins with the beauty of its capital Guadalajara and ends with the extraordinary variety and quality of its gastronomy. Homeland of the legendary musician Vicente Fernandez, who with his music makes any celebration or situation a little more pleasant and bearable. Likewise, the homeland of tequila, which has received various names around the world.

Mazatlan

Mazatlan is beautiful beaches, warm water and nights full of life. Enjoy its beaches, Olas Altas is ideal for surfers or take advantage of Sea Doo and its gentle waves. Take a walk along the Malecón, whether you go by bike or walking, it is beautiful at any time of the day. Definitely, mazatlan is one of the thousands of places in all of mexico where you can find views of the sea.

Chiapas

Chiapas is one of the most beautiful places you can visit in all of Mexico, within this state you can find the town of San Cristobal de las Casas, which is an excellent place to have a good cup of coffee, or eat a delicious pizza accompanied of a good wine. Also, a few kilometers away, is the sumidero canyon, one of the largest and best national parks in all of mexico, you can explore it in two ways, through the viewpoints, which are places where you can see the canyon from the top, or well, you can venture on a boat ride, and look at the imposing mountains on both sides of the river that crosses the canyon
